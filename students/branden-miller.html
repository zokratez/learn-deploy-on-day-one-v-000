Biography

A lot of us probably grew up watching cartoons and eating cereal in our undies on Saturday morning, so I'll fast forward a lot of years. I grew up breaking things in the house, including the new computer and Nintendo, and having to figure out how to fix it before my parents came home. Yep, I deleted tons of system files sometimes by my curiosity and sometimes pure mistake. I knew I would be in trouble if they found out I broke the computer. While they were gone, I would walk down to the public library and read books, hoping I'd find the solution to the computer problem. Through the initial fear of getting grounded, I learned how to learn on my own. This skill translated into many other areas of my life. Since then, I've become a musician/music producer, and now I'm adding to my skill set to become a Full-Stack Developer. Hopefully I can combine my creative and musical skills, with what I'm learning from Learn.co to make amazing things.
